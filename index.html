About Us

Green energy initiatives are important to reduce the negative effects of fossil-fuels on the environment.

We are committed to guiding homeowners through the myriad of available clean energy programs and incentives. Our goal is to not only promote these eco-friendly initiatives but to make them accessible and understandable for every homeowner. By providing tailored solutions, we aim to empower you in making meaningful contributions to environmental conservation. Our efforts focus on helping each individual play a part in preserving the beauty and health of our planet. Through this, we hope to foster a community that actively participates in sustainable practices, ensuring the longevity and well-being of our Earth for future generations.

Our Goals for the Future


  • 01 Increase the use of solar power across the U.S.

    If only 10% of all 1.9 Billion acres of land in the U.S. use solar panels, our carbon footprint can be reduced by 20 billion tons. If this is achieved, the average cost of $185 per ton per household to maintain carbon emissions in the US can be lowered by 30%, allowing households to pay a maximum of $51 per household per year.

  • 02 Community Education initiatives

    We are initiating programs aimed at educating local communities about green energy solutions. Our objective is to enlighten and inspire community members by providing comprehensive knowledge on eco-friendly alternatives. These educational initiatives are designed to empower individuals to adopt sustainable practices in their daily lives. Through these programs, we strive to create a ripple effect of environmental awareness and action, leading to a more sustainable and ecologically conscious society.

  • 03 Pioneer the shift from fossil fuels to clean energy.

    The shift towards clean energy, while resulting in the loss of about 5 million fossil fuel jobs by 2030, is expected to create approximately 14 million new jobs in the clean energy sector. This transition signifies a net increase of 9 million jobs, indicating a significant shift in the global job market towards more sustainable energy sources. This change not only represents a move towards environmental sustainability but also highlights the potential for substantial economic growth and diverse job opportunities in the renewable energy sector.
